**# Indian Job Market Analysis**

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) Project

**1. Executive Summary**

The Indian job market is dynamic, skill-driven, and highly competitive. Rapid digital transformation, evolving skill requirements, and sectoral growth have significantly influenced hiring patterns across industries.

This project presents a comprehensive Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of an Indian job market dataset sourced from Kaggle. The objective of this analysis is to uncover hiring trends, skill demand patterns, salary distributions, and geographic job concentration to generate actionable business insights.

As a Data & Business Analyst, I focused on transforming raw job listing data into structured intelligence that can support workforce planning, career strategy development, and talent acquisition decision-making.

**2. Business Context & Problem Statement**

Recruitment platforms, HR departments, training institutes, and policymakers rely on labor market data to understand:

- Which industries are hiring aggressively?

- What skills are in highest demand?

- How do salaries vary across roles and locations?

- Which cities dominate employment opportunities?

- What experience levels are most sought after?

However, raw job listing data does not directly provide clarity without structured analysis.

This project addresses these business questions and converts raw employment data into meaningful insights that can support:

- Talent acquisition strategies

- Career guidance decisions

- Salary benchmarking

- Skill development planning

- Workforce trend analysis

**6. Exploratory Data Analysis & Key Findings**

**Industry Hiring Trends**

The analysis examined which sectors dominate job postings. This provides insight into:

- High-growth industries

- Expanding employment sectors

- Economic activity concentration

Such insights are useful for workforce planning and career guidance.

**Location-Based Job Distribution**

The dataset was analyzed to determine cities with the highest job concentration.

This helps identify:

- Employment hubs

- Regional economic strength

- Migration and relocation trends

This insight supports strategic hiring and expansion decisions.

**Salary Distribution Analysis**

Salary ranges were analyzed to understand:

- Average compensation levels

- High-paying roles

- Salary variation across industries and locations

- Experience-based salary growth patterns

This supports salary benchmarking and compensation planning.

**Experience Level Demand**

The distribution of experience requirements was studied to identify:

- Entry-level hiring trends

- Mid-level and senior-level demand

- Market maturity indicators

This is especially useful for career planning and HR strategy.

**Skills Demand Analysis**

- Skill frequency analysis was performed to identify:

- Most in-demand technical skills

- Emerging competencies

- Cross-industry skill overlap

This provides actionable insight for:

- Training institutes

- Professionals planning upskilling

- HR teams refining job descriptions

**Correlation & Numerical Insights**

Correlation analysis was conducted to explore relationships between salary, experience, and other numerical factors.

This builds the foundation for future predictive modeling and salary estimation frameworks.
